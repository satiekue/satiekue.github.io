Local Attractions

Kona is a beautiful town, but even here in Keauhou, there are hidden gems worth exploring! These are some of our favorites.

Cultural Heritage Walk

- Explore the cultural heritage points at Keauhou Outrigger. Check out the cultural-walking-tour-map for Outrigger Kona Resort & Spa Walk out of the resort, and at Kamehameha III Road turn right, and walk down to the beach. Continue then on Kaleiopapa St. Take the first right on Ehukai St. and you're at the Outrigger. It is a beautiful resort to explore with lovely gardens and historical sights.

Ocean Blowhole

- If you take the lava oceanside path located just along the ocean pool north along the Kanaloa resort, you'll come to the golf course. Continue on the coast (you are allowed to walk along the golf course so long as you stay off the greens.) Before you get to the next resort, you'll see a beautiful large blowhole enclosed by a lava wall. Even on calm ocean days, you can see and feel the waves boom!

Fair Wind Cruises

- Few places in the world can rival the snorkel experience you will get on a Fair Wind day cruise. Explore the gorgeous and historically significant Kealakekua Bay. Very walkable from our condo. We've been enjoying snorkeling with the Fair Winds since 1982 as a family. If you're looking for a splurge for a day, this is it!

ResortPass

- Although a day at the ocean pool is hard to beat, if you want the resort experience for a day, explore the options to spend a day at Big Island resort. Options through Resort Pass include Hilton Waikoloa Village, Fairmont Orchid, Waikoloa Beach Marriot Resort & Spa, Outrigger Kona Resort & Spa
